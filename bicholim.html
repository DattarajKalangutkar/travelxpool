Bicholim Taluka

Bicholim

Mining is the word that comes most common in Bicholim Taluka people, which is the most famous taluka in Goa state. As mining serves the major role or occupation in Bicholim taluka people, Mining has occupied around 36% of land in Bicholim Taluka and also the highest as compared with others talukas.

Bicholim Taluka is not bounded by any beach like Bardez or Pernem, but still, it is famous for lots of it culture-wise and also the lakes, waterfall and other monuments which attracts the local people or tourist people to visit this wonderful taluka. Bicholim can be also known as DIVCHAL or DICHOLI by many other people. For North Goa people, Bichholim serves them the Municipal Council.

Like Pernem taluka, Bicholim Taluka also has borders touch with the Maharashtra border which also serves as the entry gate for the Maharashtra people to visit Goa. Its unique Culture, festivals like "Ghode Modni", Fair (Jatra) like "Shirgao Zatra", "Kelbai zatra", "Mahashivrati" at Pandav caves, etc which truly attracts or inspired to visit this taluka.

Mayem
There are many beautiful villages present in the taluka, one of them is Mayem Village which is full of hidden treasure like the famous Mayem lake, hidden waterfalls, the famous Kelbai Temple which is the elder sister of the famous goddess of Shri Larai Devi whose temple are situated in her neighborhood village which is Shirago. Many tourists visit this place if they are in the bicholim taluka for no doubt. Now Tourism of Goa had introduced a Bungee jump to attract more visitors to visit this place. For Every local people, Mayem Lake serves as the picnic spot where the beautiful Garder and the boat ride make the people's hearts full of Joy.

Sankhalim
TIf anyone asks, what is there famous in sankhalim Village, No one can be denied the word Temple, because In Sankhalim Village there are a lot of beautiful and most historic temples. There is Ganesh temple, Mahadev temple, Datta Mandir Temple, Radha-Krishna Temple, Maruti Temple, Saiba Temple around the area of Sankhalim. Besides Temple, Sankhalim Village also has a famous Ravindra Bhagwan Hall. The Famous Management Institute that Goa Institute of Management is also a famous institute present in the landmark of Sankhalim Villages.
Shirgao
Shirgao is another famous village of Bicholim Taluka. It is famous for the Shri lairai Devi Temple all around the Goa state. People from all other Taluka come to take blessing from the goddess Lairai Devi on the occasion on the Lairai Zatra which mostly happened in April or May. There are lots of stories behind this famous Goddess which people take interest to listen and even Grandmother or grandparent they tell the story to their children with great interest.
