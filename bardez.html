Bardez Taluka

Bardez

Bardez Taluka was said to be the municipality at the time of Portuguese time. The word Bardez has a meaning as Bara means as "Twelve" and desh as "countries". In this, Countries refer to the twelve Comunidades such as Serula, Siolim,Aldona,Anjuna,Assagao,Candolim,Moira, Nachinola,Olaulim,Pomburpa,Saligao,Sangolda

Bardez Taluka is not famous for its beaches but also great heritages.

Like Pernem Taluka, Bardez taluka is also bounded by rivers, such on the north it is bounded by chapora river, south by Mandovi river, and east is bounded by Mapusa rivers.

Now let us talk about the Beaches present in the Bardez Taluka.

  • Candolim beach

    Candolim Beach is famous for its restaurants and pubs.

  • Sinquerim Beach

    Sinquerim beach is situated between Candolim and Aguada fort. Hotel Taj is in backdrop accompanied by Aguada fort.

  • Calangute Beach

    Calangute beach is one of the most famous places in goa to visit. Lots of famous restaurants and pubs are present here.

  • Baga Beach

    Baga Beach is another famous beach of goa, attached along with Calangute beach many people visit here in large number.

  • Anjuna Beach

    Anjuna beach is amongst the famous beaches of Goa.

  • Vagator Beach

    Vagator Beach has a great landscape. It is famous for picnics among locals.

As you stay in every taluka, the Local people are known by the taluka name, so in Bardez, the people staying Bardez are called as BARDEZCAR in know to know his/her identity to rest of the talukas. The word MAPUSA is incomplete when heard or listen to anything related to Bardez Taluka.

Mapusa
The word MAPUSA is incomplete when heard or listen to anything related to Bardez Taluka.
Mapusa or mapuca is the word used by the Goan and Portuguese people to describe the beauty of this town. Mapusa is a headquarters in the Bardez taluka. The distance between Mapusa to the capital city of Goa which is Panaji is almost 13km. The famous temple such as Shree Dev Bodgeshwar Sansthan of Lord Bodgeshwar is situated on the land of Mapusa.
Besides that The Church of Our Lady of Miracles is also situated in Mapusa. Besides Temple and Churches, the Famous Friday market held at Mapusa is entirely through the day look like there is great fair in the place. The position of every section of the category in the market is well organized and well placed at the time of Portuguese.
In the Mapusa market, the placement of every category of products, whether it is fruits or fish or vegetables or clothes, etc are placed in the order so that visitors won't like confused about whether we're to get in first. In spite of having limited tourist sites, Mapusa is reached with College University. The famous St. Xaviers college attract thousand of the youngster to have a qualification degree in that university.
Maruti Temple which is situated aside the Circle main Road attracts the tourist Peoples.
